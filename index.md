# Mob Maze

## Game Overview
Mob Maze is an exciting adventure game where players navigate through a labyrinth filled with various challenges and obstacles. The ultimate goal is to reach the end of the maze while overcoming enemies and solving puzzles.

## Features
- **Challenging Mazes:** Each maze is uniquely designed with increasing difficulty levels.
- **Dynamic Enemies:** Players encounter different types of mobs with unique behaviors and attack patterns.
- **Power-ups:** Collect power-ups that offer temporary boosts to speed, strength, or defense.
- **Leaderboards:** Compete with friends and other players to achieve the best time in completing levels.

## Gameplay Mechanics
- **Movement:** Players can move up, down, left, or right to navigate the maze.
- **Combat:** Engage in combat with mobs using various weapons and tools found throughout the maze.
- **Puzzles:** Some areas of the maze will require players to solve puzzles to unlock new paths.

## Guides
- **Getting Started:** Learn the basics of movement, combat, and item collection.
- **Tips for Navigating Mazes:** Strategies for finding the quickest routes.
- **Combat Strategies:** Best practices for fighting each type of enemy.

## Strategies
- **Map Awareness:** Always be aware of your surroundings; use the maze layout to your advantage.
- **Resource Management:** Keep track of health and power-ups to ensure survival during difficult encounters.
- **Practice:** Regular practice will help you master maze navigation and enemy combat.

Feel free to explore and enjoy your adventure in Mob Maze!
